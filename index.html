Deus.iA — Support

Official help and contact page for the Deus.iA app.

Contact

For questions, technical issues, or help requests, email:

deus.ai.support@gmail.com

We typically reply on business days. Include your app version and platform (iOS or Android) in your message.

Frequently asked questions

I forgot my password

In the app, use the password recovery option on the sign-in screen and follow the link sent to your email.

Purchases or subscription

Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store). For refunds or billing issues, use your Apple purchase history or email us with your receipt.

Delete my account

Email deus.ai.support@gmail.com from the address associated with the account you want removed. We may ask for confirmation for security reasons.

Report a bug or suggestion

Write to deus.ai.support@gmail.com with a description of what happened and, if possible, screenshots.
